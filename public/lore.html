FRIDGE WITCH

long ago, the fridge witch was born from the last pickle in the jar...

With a whisper and a waft of garlic air, she began conjuring meals from misfit leftovers. Her spellbook doesn’t contain recipes, but rather rules bent by instinct and joy. She believes true magic is not in gourmet perfection, but in surprising harmony. If your fridge looks cursed, fear not — she sees potential where others see chaos.
